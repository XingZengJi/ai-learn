## What You'll Learn 你将学到什么

This video introduces how the AI Fluency Framework applies specifically to educators. We explore how the 4Ds (Delegation, Description, Discernment, Diligence) can transform your teaching practice through thoughtful AI collaboration. We discuss how AI can become a thinking partner for course design, material creation, and assessment development while maintaining the balance between human expertise and AI assistance. The video emphasizes that being AI Fluent includes knowing when and how to collaborate with AI and always keeping pedagogical values at the center. We also address why AI Fluency in education matters now: Students are already using AI, employers expect AI fluency, and we have the timely opportunity to model thoughtful engagement with these technologies.

> 本课视频介绍 AI Fluency(AI 流畅度)框架如何专门应用于教育者。我们会探讨 4D——委派(Delegation)、描述(Description)、辨别(Discernment)、审慎(Diligence)——如何通过有思考的 AI 协作,改变你的教学实践。我们会讨论 AI 如何能成为课程设计、材料制作和评估开发的「思考伙伴」,同时在人的专业判断与 AI 辅助之间保持平衡。视频强调,具备 AI 流畅度包括知道何时、如何与 AI 协作,并始终把教学价值观放在核心位置。我们也会说明为什么此刻教育领域的 AI 流畅度如此重要:学生已经在使用 AI,雇主期待员工具备 AI 流畅度,而我们正好有一个及时的机会,去示范如何审慎地运用这些技术。

对产品经理背景的你来说,可以把这里的「思考伙伴」理解成:AI 不是替你把课备好、把作业判完的「外包团队」,而更像一个随时能对话、帮你把想法想得更透的「资深同事」——真正拍板的仍然是你,AI 负责陪你把方案打磨得更扎实。

## Key Takeaways 核心要点

- **The 4D Framework can help educators build genuine thinking partnerships with AI on the foundation of pedagogical integrity.**

  **4D 框架能帮助教育者在教学诚信(pedagogical integrity)的基础上,与 AI 建立真正的思考伙伴关系。**

- **Context-building is essential, as AI becomes more helpful when it understands your specific situation, values, and constraints.**

  **构建上下文(context-building)至关重要——当 AI 理解你的具体情境、价值观和限制条件时,它才能提供真正有用的帮助。**

- **AI Fluency for educators means enhancing rather than replacing human expertise and judgment in teaching and learning.**

  **对教育者而言,AI 流畅度意味着「增强」而非「取代」教学与学习中人的专业判断。**

## Exercises 练习

### Exercise: Building a Shared Teaching Vision (30 minutes) 练习:构建共享的教学愿景(30 分钟)

This foundational exercise is good for fostering reflection and creates a reusable artifact for future AI collaborations.

> 这是一个基础性练习,有助于促进自我反思,并会产出一份可在未来 AI 协作中反复使用的成果文档。

#### Part 1: Self-Reflection (10 minutes) 第一部分:自我反思(10 分钟)

Before engaging with AI, clarify your own position:

> 在与 AI 互动之前,先厘清自己的立场:

- What are your core teaching values and philosophy? 你的核心教学价值观与教育理念是什么?
- What unique constraints do you face (institutional, technological, time)? 你面临哪些独特的限制(机构层面、技术层面、时间层面)?
- What are your students typically like (background, challenges, goals)? 你的学生通常是什么样的(背景、面临的挑战、目标)?
- What teaching methods/learning experiences do you most value and why? 你最看重哪些教学方法/学习体验?为什么?

#### Part 2: Discuss Your Teaching Values and Context (25 minutes) 第二部分:与 AI 讨论你的教学价值观和情境(25 分钟)

Start a conversation with Claude (or any other AI assistant you choose):

> 与 Claude(或你选择的任何其他 AI 助手)开启一段对话:

**Opening the conversation 开启对话**

- Explain that you're an educator who wants to build a reusable summary of their teaching approach and context.

  说明你是一名教育者,希望构建一份关于自己教学方法与情境的、可复用的总结。

- Let the AI know this document will help establish shared understanding for future collaborations.

  告诉 AI,这份文档将用于在未来的协作中建立共同理解。

- Indicate you'd like the AI to help you think through all relevant aspects of your teaching practice.

  表明你希望 AI 帮你梳理清楚教学实践中所有相关的方面。

- You can ask the AI to interview you about your teaching, OR you can just start sharing information and ask the AI what else would be helpful to know.

  你可以让 AI 像采访一样向你提问,或者你也可以直接开始分享信息,再让 AI 告诉你还需要补充哪些内容才有帮助。

Take your time. The goal is a back-and-forth conversation that surfaces important context.

> 不用着急。目标是通过一来一回的对话,把重要的情境信息挖掘出来。

**Key areas to cover (the AI should help you explore these) 需要覆盖的关键领域(AI 应帮你逐一探讨)**

- Your subject area, level, and specific courses 你所教授的学科、学段和具体课程
- Typical challenges faced by your students 学生通常面临的困难
- Institutional context and constraints 所在机构的情境与限制条件
- Your teaching philosophy and preferred methods 你的教学理念与偏好的方法
- Common pain points in your teaching 你教学中常见的痛点
- Goals for integrating AI into your practice 将 AI 融入教学实践的目标
- Any unique aspects of your teaching situation 你教学情境中任何独特之处

**Make it conversational 让对话自然进行**

- Don't feel you need to share everything at once.

  不必觉得要一次性把所有内容都说完。

- Encourage the AI to ask follow-up questions.

  鼓励 AI 提出追问。

- Share specific examples when they help illustrate your context (be aware of privacy and sensitive data).

  在有助于说明情境时分享具体例子(注意隐私与敏感数据)。

- It's okay to think out loud and refine your thoughts as you go.

  边说边想、逐步完善想法,完全没问题。

**Creating a reusable document 生成可复用的文档**

- Ask the AI to synthesize the conversation into a structured context document you can share in future AI conversations.

  请 AI 把这次对话整理成一份结构化的情境文档,方便你在未来的 AI 对话中直接分享使用。

- Review it together and add anything missing and/or make corrections.

  和 AI 一起检查这份文档,补充遗漏、修正错误。

- Request a format that's easy to copy and reuse.

  要求生成一种方便复制和复用的格式。
