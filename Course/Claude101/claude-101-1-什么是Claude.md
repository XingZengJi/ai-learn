## What is Claude? 什么是 Claude?(10 分钟)

Claude is more than a chatbot—it's an AI assistant designed to be your thinking partner. In this lesson you'll learn what makes Claude different from other AI tools and see how it can help with a wide variety of work tasks.

> Claude 不仅仅是一个聊天机器人——它是一款被设计成你思考伙伴的 AI 助手。在本课中,你将了解 Claude 与其他 AI 工具的不同之处,并看到它如何帮助完成各种各样的工作任务。

### Key takeaways 关键要点

- Claude is built to be helpful, harmless, and honest: At a high level, Claude is guided by principles that help it avoid toxic or discriminatory outputs, avoid helping humans engage in illegal or unethical activities, and broadly behave as a helpful, honest, and harmless AI system. This approach, called Constitutional AI, means Claude is trained to align with human values and operate transparently.

  Claude 的设计宗旨是乐于助人、无害且诚实:从宏观层面看,Claude 遵循一系列原则,这些原则帮助它避免输出有害或歧视性内容,避免协助人类从事非法或不道德的活动,并总体上表现为一个乐于助人、诚实且无害的 AI 系统。这种方法被称为「宪法式 AI」(Constitutional AI),意味着 Claude 经过训练,能够与人类价值观保持一致,并以透明的方式运作。

- Claude is more than a chatbot: Claude is capable of a wide variety of conversational and text processing tasks while maintaining a high degree of reliability and predictability, including summarization, search, creative and collaborative writing, Q&A, coding, and more. Think of Claude as a thought partner who can help you tackle complex problems, and work through challenging situations, not just answer simple questions.

  Claude 不仅仅是一个聊天机器人:Claude 能够处理种类繁多的对话与文本任务,同时保持高度的可靠性与可预测性,包括摘要、搜索、创意与协作写作、问答、编程等等。可以把 Claude 看作一个思维伙伴,它不仅能回答简单问题,还能帮你解决复杂问题、应对充满挑战的情境。

- Claude is designed to be steerable and collaborative: Claude can take direction on personality, tone, and behavior, and customers report that Claude is much less likely to produce harmful outputs, easier to converse with, and more steerable—so you can get your desired output with less effort.

  Claude 的设计注重可引导性与协作性:Claude 能够根据指示调整个性、语气和行为,客户反馈显示 Claude 产生有害输出的可能性大大降低、对话更顺畅、也更容易引导——因此你能用更少的精力获得理想的输出结果。

- You can access Claude wherever you work: Claude apps are available to all plan types—Free, Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise. Your conversations, projects, memory, and preferences sync across all devices when you're signed in. Whether you're at your desk or on the go, Claude is available through web, desktop, and mobile apps.

  无论你在哪里工作,都能使用 Claude:Claude 应用适用于所有套餐类型——免费版、专业版(Pro)、Max 版、团队版(Team)和企业版(Enterprise)。登录后,你的对话、项目、记忆和偏好设置会在所有设备间同步。无论你是在办公桌前还是在外奔波,都可以通过网页端、桌面端和移动端应用使用 Claude。

## Understanding Claude's capabilities 理解 Claude 的能力

Claude can help with a wide range of tasks that go far beyond simple question-and-answer interactions to assistant-like partnership that can both automate and augment your work. Here's a few things Claude excels at:

> Claude 能够协助完成范围极广的任务,远远超出简单的问答互动,形成一种既能自动化、又能增强你工作的助手式伙伴关系。以下是 Claude 擅长的一些领域:

- **Writing and content creation**: Claude can collaborate with you on social media posts, professional emails, and complex reports. Because Claude is trained to take direction on personality and tone, you can iterate together on structure and clarity until your voice comes through clearly.

  **写作与内容创作**:Claude 可以与你协作完成社交媒体帖子、专业邮件和复杂报告。由于 Claude 经过训练能够根据指示调整个性与语气,你可以和它反复打磨结构与清晰度,直到你的个人风格清晰地体现出来。

- **Research and analysis**: Claude helps you explore research angles, compile findings, and analyze data to surface meaningful insights. You can upload documents and Claude will help you make sense of complex information—this is enabled by Claude's large context window, which can ingest 200K+ tokens (about 500 pages of text or more), with up to 1M tokens available on Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans when using supported models. This allows Claude to consider extensive materials in a single conversation.

  **研究与分析**:Claude 能帮助你探索研究角度、整合发现、分析数据,从而提炼出有意义的洞见。你可以上传文档,Claude 会帮你理清复杂信息——这得益于 Claude 庞大的上下文窗口,可处理超过 20 万 token(约 500 页文本或更多);在 Pro、Max、Team 及 Enterprise 套餐中使用受支持模型时,上下文容量最高可达 100 万 token。这使得 Claude 能在单次对话中处理大量材料。

- **Coding assistance**: Coding is one of Claude's greatest strengths. Claude's strong performance on real-world coding tasks means it can help you write, debug, and explain code across multiple programming languages.

  **编码辅助**:编码是 Claude 最突出的强项之一。Claude 在真实编码任务中的出色表现,使它能够帮你跨多种编程语言编写、调试和讲解代码。

- **Problem-solving and reasoning**: Claude handles complex cognitive tasks, mathematical problems, strategic thinking and analysis, and research. Claude's recent Opus and Sonnet models are hybrid models offering two modes: near-instant responses and extended thinking for deeper reasoning. Anthropic Extended thinking allows Claude to work through problems step-by-step, making it well-suited for tasks that require careful analysis.

  **问题解决与推理**:Claude 能处理复杂的认知任务、数学问题、战略思考与分析,以及研究工作。Claude 最新的 Opus 与 Sonnet 模型是混合模型,提供两种模式:近乎即时的响应,以及用于深度推理的扩展思考(extended thinking)。Anthropic 的扩展思考功能让 Claude 能够逐步推演问题,因而特别适合需要细致分析的任务。

- **Learning new things**: Whether you're learning a new skill, exploring unfamiliar domains, or working through complex challenges, Claude can adapt to your learning style and pace. Learning mode is a new Claude experience that guides your reasoning process rather than providing answers, helping develop critical thinking skills.

  **学习新事物**:无论你是在学习新技能、探索陌生领域,还是应对复杂挑战,Claude 都能适应你的学习风格和节奏。学习模式(Learning mode)是 Claude 全新推出的一种体验,它引导你的推理过程而非直接给出答案,帮助你培养批判性思维能力。

Get inspired on ways to use Claude in your specific function by exploring the use-case gallery on claude.com. For a deeper dive into what AI can (and can't) do, see the AI Capabilities course.

> 想获得针对你所在职能的使用灵感,可以浏览 claude.com 上的用例展示库。想更深入了解 AI 能做什么、不能做什么,可以学习 Anthropic 的「AI 能力」(AI Capabilities)课程。

## Ways to access Claude 访问 Claude 的方式

Claude is the intelligence—the AI assistant you're learning to work with throughout this course. That same intelligence is available across multiple interfaces, each suited to different types of tasks.

> Claude 是这套智能本身——也就是你在本课程中学习与之协作的 AI 助手。这套智能可以通过多种界面访问,每种界面都适合不同类型的任务。

- **Claude.ai** (and the associated mobile and desktop apps) are the primary way most people interact with Claude. Here, you can ask questions, brainstorm ideas, create and edit documents, and a lot more. Claude.ai is ideal for conversations, writing assistance, research, analysis, and creating files. This is the focus of this course.

  **Claude.ai**(及其配套的移动端和桌面端应用)是大多数人使用 Claude 的主要方式。在这里,你可以提问、集思广益、创建和编辑文档,以及做更多事情。Claude.ai 非常适合对话、写作辅助、研究、分析和文件创建,也是本课程的重点内容。

- **Claude Code** is an agentic coding tool that is designed for developers but can be used for all kinds of file manipulation on your desktop. Claude Code can directly edit files, run commands, and create commits.

  **Claude Code** 是一款面向开发者设计的智能体编码工具,但也可以用于桌面端各种文件操作。Claude Code 能够直接编辑文件、运行命令并创建提交。

- **@Claude** brings Claude directly into Slack. You can chat with Claude in the AI assistant header from any channel or conversation, or by tagging @Claude in threads. When you connect Slack to Claude, Claude searches your workspace's channels, direct messages, and shared files to find the context you need for better responses and research.

  **@Claude(Slack)**:把 Claude 直接带入 Slack。你可以在任意频道或对话的 AI 助手标题栏中与 Claude 聊天,或者在讨论串中标记 @Claude 来互动。当你把 Slack 连接到 Claude 后,Claude 会搜索你工作区的频道、私信和共享文件,找到所需的上下文,从而给出更好的回复并支持研究。

- **Claude Design** is a dedicated space for turning ideas into working interfaces. Describe what you want—or start from a sketch or screenshot—and Claude builds an interactive prototype you can refine and hand off to your team.

  **Claude Design**:一个专门用来把创意转化为可用界面的空间。描述你想要的效果——或者从一张草图、截图开始——Claude 就会构建出一个交互式原型,供你完善后交给团队。

- **Claude for Microsoft 365** brings Claude into Excel, PowerPoint, Word, and Outlook as a sidebar, so you can analyze, draft, and edit inside the document you already have open—and carry context from one app to the next.

  **Claude for Microsoft 365**:以侧边栏的形式把 Claude 带入 Excel、PowerPoint、Word 和 Outlook,让你可以直接在已经打开的文档中进行分析、起草和编辑——并让上下文在不同应用之间延续。

This course will focus primarily on Claude.ai, but you can also check out Claude Code in Action for more information on using Claude in development workflows.

> 本课程将主要聚焦于 Claude.ai,但你也可以查看「Claude Code 实战」(Claude Code in Action)课程,了解如何在开发工作流中使用 Claude 的更多信息。
