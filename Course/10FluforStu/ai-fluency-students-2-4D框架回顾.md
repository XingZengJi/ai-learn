## Lesson Content 课程内容

This comprehensive video introduces the 4D Framework—Delegation, Description, Discernment, and Diligence—as the foundation of AI fluency. It emphasizes that AI fluency is about augmentation (adding to your work) rather than automation (having AI do work for you), which is especially important in learning contexts. The video explores each D in detail: Delegation involves problem awareness, platform awareness, and task delegation; Description covers product, process, and performance aspects of communication; Discernment examines critical evaluation at all three levels; and Diligence ensures responsible, transparent, and accountable AI use. The video highlights how Description and Discernment work together in a continuous feedback loop, transforming AI from a tool into a thinking partner.

> 这段内容详尽的视频介绍了 4D 框架——委派(Delegation)、描述(Description)、辨别(Discernment)、审慎(Diligence)——作为 AI 流畅度的基础。视频强调,AI 流畅度关乎「增强」(augmentation,为你的工作添砖加瓦),而非「自动化」(automation,让 AI 替你把活干了),这一点在学习情境中尤其重要。视频详细拆解了每一个 D:委派涉及问题意识、平台意识和任务委派;描述涵盖沟通中「产出物(product)」「过程(process)」「表现方式(performance)」三个方面;辨别则在这三个层面上都需要进行批判性评估;审慎确保 AI 的使用是负责任的、透明的、可问责的。视频重点指出,描述与辨别是如何在一个持续的反馈循环中协同运作的,从而把 AI 从一个「工具」转变为一个「思考伙伴」。

This video is just an introduction to the framework, for the full breakdown and many practical exercises, check out our AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations course here.

> 这段视频只是对该框架的简要介绍,完整的详细拆解以及大量实践练习,请参见我们的《AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations》课程。

## Key Takeaways 核心要点

- **The 4Ds work together to create genuine AI Fluency.**

  **4D 协同运作,共同构成真正的 AI 流畅度。**

- **Augmentation (working with AI) is more valuable than automation in the learning context (AI doing work for you).**

  **在学习情境中,「增强」(与 AI 协作)比「自动化」(让 AI 替你把活干了)更有价值。**

- **Description and Discernment form a continuous improvement loop.**

  **描述与辨别构成一个持续改进的循环。**

- **These competencies remain relevant regardless of how AI technology evolves.**

  **无论 AI 技术如何演进,这些能力都始终适用。**

## Exercises 练习

This foundational exercise helps you establish your learning context and goals, creating a reusable document for future AI collaborations. Always be careful when sharing information with an AI (or any computer system). Protect your privacy and the privacy of others.

> 这个基础性练习能帮你确立自己的学习情境和目标,产出一份可在未来 AI 协作中反复使用的文档。与 AI(或任何计算机系统)分享信息时,请务必谨慎——保护好自己和他人的隐私。

### Step 1: Self-Reflection (5 minutes) 第一步:自我反思(5 分钟)

Before engaging with AI, clarify your own position as a learner. Consider your:

> 在与 AI 互动之前,先厘清自己作为学习者的处境。想一想:

**Academic context 学业情境**

- What are you studying and at what level (major, year, specific courses)? 你在学什么、处于什么阶段(专业、年级、具体课程)?
- What are your strongest and weakest subject areas? 你最擅长和最薄弱的学科分别是什么?
- What types of assignments do you typically work on? 你通常要完成哪些类型的作业?
- What are your academic goals this term and beyond? 这学期乃至更长远的学业目标是什么?

**Learning style and challenges 学习风格与挑战**

- How do you learn best (visual, verbal, hands-on, etc.)? 你用哪种方式学习效果最好(视觉型、语言型、动手实践型等)?
- What aspects of learning do you find most challenging? 学习中哪些方面对你来说最有挑战?
- Where do you typically need the most help or support? 你通常最需要在哪些地方获得帮助或支持?
- What motivates you to learn and persist through difficulties? 是什么在激励你学习、在遇到困难时坚持下去?

**AI experience and goals AI 使用经验与目标**

- What experience do you already have with AI tools? 你已经有哪些使用 AI 工具的经验?
- What concerns do you have about using AI in your studies? 在学业中使用 AI,你有哪些顾虑?
- What do you hope AI can help you achieve academically? 你希望 AI 能帮你在学业上达成什么?
- What boundaries do you want to set for AI use in your learning? 在学习中使用 AI,你想为自己设定哪些边界?

### Step 2: Creating Your Learning Context Document with AI (15 minutes) 第二步:与 AI 一起创建你的学习情境文档(15 分钟)

Start a conversation with Claude (or your preferred AI assistant):

> 与 Claude(或你偏好的其他 AI 助手)开启一段对话:

**Opening the conversation 开启对话**

- Explain that you're a student building a learning context document.

  说明你是一名学生,正在构建一份学习情境文档。

- Tell the AI this will help establish how you can work together effectively on academic tasks.

  告诉 AI,这份文档将帮助确立你们在学业任务上该如何有效协作。

- Make it clear that you want to use AI to enhance your learning, not replace it.

  明确表示你希望用 AI 来「增强」自己的学习,而不是「替代」学习。

**Key areas to explore with the AI 需要与 AI 一起探讨的关键领域**

- Your current courses and learning challenges 你目前的课程和学习中遇到的困难
- Your goals and what success looks like for you 你的目标,以及对你来说「成功」是什么样子
- Your preferred study methods and what helps you understand concepts 你偏好的学习方法,以及什么方式能帮你真正理解概念
- Situations where you want AI support versus where you want to work independently 哪些情况下你想要 AI 支持,哪些情况下你想独立完成
- Your school's AI policies and how to work within them 你所在学校的 AI 使用政策,以及如何在这些政策范围内工作

**Building the document together 一起构建这份文档**

- Share the reflections from Step 1 with the AI.

  把第一步的反思内容分享给 AI。

- Let the AI ask follow-up questions to understand your context better.

  让 AI 提出追问,以便更好地理解你的情境。

- Be specific about examples of assignments or concepts you're working on.

  具体举例说明你正在做的作业或正在学习的概念。

- Discuss what kind of AI support would actually help you learn versus just complete tasks.

  讨论清楚:什么样的 AI 支持才是真正帮你学习,而不只是帮你把任务完成。

**Important boundaries to establish 需要确立的重要边界**

- Make it clear you want to learn, not have work done for you.

  明确表示你想要的是学习,而不是让 AI 替你把作业做完。

- Discuss how you'll maintain academic integrity.

  讨论你将如何维护学术诚信。

- Establish when you'll work with AI versus independently.

  明确什么时候你会借助 AI、什么时候你会独立完成。

- Set expectations for the type of help you want (guidance, practice, feedback, etc.).

  设定你想要的帮助类型的预期(引导、练习、反馈等)。

### Step 3: Finalizing Your Document (5 minutes) 第三步:完善你的文档(5 分钟)

**Creating your reference document 创建你的参考文档**

- Ask the AI to synthesize your discussion into a structured learning context document.

  请 AI 把你们的讨论整理成一份结构化的学习情境文档。

- Review it together and make any necessary adjustments.

  一起检查这份文档,做必要的调整。

- Include a section on "How I Want to Work with AI" based on your boundaries.

  基于你设定的边界,加入一个「我希望如何与 AI 协作」的部分。

- Save this document to share at the start of future AI learning sessions.

  保存这份文档,以便未来每次与 AI 进行学习相关对话时,一开始就分享给它。

**Reflection questions 反思问题**

- What surprised you about articulating your learning needs? 把自己的学习需求说清楚这件事,有哪些地方让你感到意外?
- How do you think having this context will improve your AI interactions? 你觉得有了这份情境文档,会如何改善你和 AI 的互动?
