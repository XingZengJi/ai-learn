## Lesson Content 课程内容

This video explores what it means to be "the human in the loop"—the decision-maker who steers AI interactions with judgment, creativity, and ethics. It emphasizes that being human in the loop means you decide what to ask, evaluate whether AI outputs make sense, and bring your values to every interaction. The video encourages creating a personal AI collaboration policy that includes when you'll engage with AI (and when you won't), how you'll use it for learning, transparency commitments, and strategies for keeping skills sharp. It stresses that continuous reflection on AI practice is crucial, asking questions like: Is this helping me grow? Am I developing needed skills? Am I being honest about AI's role? The video concludes by highlighting that your humanity—your specific talents, experiences, and perspectives—remains uniquely valuable and irreplaceable.

> 本课视频探讨「作为闭环中的人类(the human in the loop)」意味着什么——也就是那个用判断力、创造力和伦理意识来引导 AI 互动的决策者。视频强调,「作为闭环中的人类」意味着:由你决定该问什么问题、由你评估 AI 的输出是否合理,并且在每一次互动中都带入你自己的价值观。视频鼓励你创建一份个人 AI 协作政策,其中包括:什么时候你会使用 AI(以及什么时候不会)、你打算如何把 AI 用于学习、你对透明度的承诺,以及保持自身能力不退化的策略。视频强调,对自己使用 AI 的实践进行持续反思至关重要,要不断追问这样的问题:这样做真的在帮助我成长吗?我是否在培养所需要的能力?我对 AI 所扮演的角色是否足够诚实?视频最后指出,你的「人性」——你特有的才华、经历和视角——始终是独一无二、不可替代的宝贵财富。

## Key Takeaways 核心要点

- **Being the "human in the loop" means maintaining decision-making control in all AI interactions.**

  **「作为闭环中的人类」意味着在所有与 AI 的互动中,始终掌握着决策的主导权。**

- **A personal commitment to responsible AI use helps navigate ethical complexities.**

  **对负责任地使用 AI 的个人承诺,能帮你应对其中复杂的伦理问题。**

- **Regular reflection on AI practice ensures continued growth and alignment with values.**

  **定期反思自己使用 AI 的实践,能确保自己持续成长,并与自身价值观保持一致。**

- **Your human insight, judgment, and care remain irreplaceable.**

  **你作为人类所拥有的洞察力、判断力和用心,始终是不可替代的。**

- **AI Fluency is a journey of continuous learning and ethical development.**

  **AI 流畅度是一段持续学习、持续进行伦理成长的旅程。**

## Exercises 练习

This comprehensive exercise helps you develop a thoughtful, practical policy for your AI use that you'll actually follow.

> 这个综合性练习会帮你制定一份经过深思熟虑、切实可行、你真的会去遵守的 AI 使用政策。

### Part 1: Foundation Building (10 minutes) 第一部分:打基础(10 分钟)

Go offline to consider everything you've learned in this course and jot down your initial thoughts.

> 先脱离 AI,自己安静地想一想这门课程里学到的一切,把最初的想法记录下来。

**Exploring your values and boundaries 探索你的价值观和边界**

Think through:

> 想清楚:

- Your core values around learning, integrity, and achievement. 你在学习、诚信、成就方面的核心价值观。
- Situations where you absolutely will not use AI (your "red lines"). 你绝对不会使用 AI 的情况(你的「红线」)。
- Areas where AI support feels appropriate and beneficial. 你觉得 AI 支持恰当且有益的领域。
- How you want others to perceive your AI use. 你希望别人如何看待你使用 AI 的方式。
- What kind of professional you want to become. 你想成为什么样的职业人士。

In addition, consider the perspectives of others:

> 此外,也考虑一下他人的视角:

- How would you want your classmates to use AI? 你希望同学们如何使用 AI?
- What would you think if your professor used AI in different ways? 如果教授以不同的方式使用 AI,你会怎么想?
- How would you want employees to use AI if you were an employer? 如果你是雇主,你希望员工如何使用 AI?
- What uses of AI would make you uncomfortable if others did them? 别人以哪些方式使用 AI,会让你感到不舒服?

### Part 2: Drafting Your Policy (20 minutes) 第二部分:起草你的政策(20 分钟)

Share this context with AI and discuss different sections of your policy (sample topics below - make them your own!):

> 把这些思考分享给 AI,讨论你这份政策的各个部分(下面是示例主题——把它们改造成属于你自己的版本!):

**Section 1: When I Will and Won't Use AI 第一节:我什么时候会用、什么时候不会用 AI**

Define clear boundaries:

> 明确清晰的边界:

- Learning situations where AI is appropriate (practice, exploration, building understanding, applying knowledge). 适合使用 AI 的学习场景(练习、探索、建立理解、应用知识)。
- Situations where I'll work independently (exams, demonstrating mastery, original thinking). 我会独立完成的场景(考试、证明自己已掌握、原创性思考)。
- Professional contexts where AI adds value. AI 能带来价值的职业情境。
- Times when human-only work is essential. 必须完全由人独立完成的时刻。

**Section 2: How I'll Use AI for Learning 第二节:我将如何用 AI 来学习**

Specify your approach:

> 明确你的方法:

- AI as tutor/coach, not homework completer. 把 AI 当作辅导老师/教练,而不是替我做作业的人。
- Maintaining ability to explain everything I submit. 始终保持对自己提交的一切内容的解释能力。
- Using AI to enhance understanding, not bypass it. 用 AI 来增强理解,而不是绕过理解。
- Specific strategies that have worked for me. 对我自己真正有效的具体策略。

**Section 3: Transparency Commitments 第三节:透明度承诺**

Define your disclosure standards:

> 明确你的披露标准:

- When I'll proactively disclose AI assistance. 我会在什么情况下主动披露 AI 提供了帮助。
- How I'll document AI contributions. 我将如何记录 AI 的具体贡献。
- What level of detail I'll provide. 我会提供多详细的说明。
- How I'll handle ambiguous situations. 遇到模糊不清的情况时我会如何处理。

**Section 4: Skill Maintenance 第四节:能力维护**

Protect your capabilities:

> 保护好自己的能力:

- Core skills I'll practice without AI. 我会不借助 AI、持续练习的核心技能。
- Regular "AI-free" work to maintain abilities. 定期进行「不用 AI」的练习,以维持能力。
- How I'll verify I'm still learning. 我将如何验证自己确实还在学习和进步。
- Signs that I'm over-relying on AI (and signs that I may be under-utilizing AI!). 我过度依赖 AI 的迹象(以及我可能对 AI 利用不足的迹象!)。

**Section 5: Ethical Guidelines 第五节:伦理准则**

Your personal ethics:

> 你的个人伦理准则:

- Accuracy and fact-checking responsibilities. 准确性与事实核查方面的责任。
- Respect for others' work and ideas. 对他人作品和想法的尊重。
- Privacy and data considerations. 隐私与数据方面的考量。
- Other impacts of AI and computer use. 使用 AI 和计算机系统带来的其他影响。

**Section 6: Continued Growth 第六节:持续成长**

Planning for the future:

> 为未来做规划:

- How I'll stay updated on AI capabilities. 我将如何持续了解 AI 能力的最新进展。
- Regular reflection intervals. 定期的反思节奏。
- Adjusting policies as I learn. 随着自己学习的深入,不断调整这份政策。
- Sharing knowledge with others. 把所学分享给他人。

### Part 3: Making It Real (5 minutes) 第三部分:让它落地(5 分钟)

Review your draft policy with the AI:

> 和 AI 一起回顾你的政策草案:

- Is this realistic given your context and constraints? 考虑到你的情境和限制条件,这份政策现实可行吗?
- How will you maintain accountability? 你将如何保持自我约束、对自己负责?
- Practice applying the policy to a few real scenarios (a course revision, an assignment, a job application, even just a little personal project). 练习把这份政策套用到几个真实场景中(一次课程复习、一份作业、一次求职申请,甚至是一个小小的个人项目)。
- Ask the AI to help format this as a clear, professional document. 请 AI 帮你把它排版成一份清晰、专业的文档。
- Keep it concise enough to actually reference. 保持简洁,让它真的能被你拿来参考。
- Include date created and plan for updates. 注明创建日期,并规划好更新计划。
- Save in multiple places for easy access. 保存在多个地方,方便随时查阅。
- Use it and maybe share it and discuss it with a friend. 实际使用它,也可以考虑分享给朋友并一起讨论。
